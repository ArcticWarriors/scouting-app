Our Robot

Design Statement

The 2016 Team 174 robot is quick and maneuverable, able to cross any driving defenses.

It is primarily designed to acquire and manipulate BOULDERS from the ground, score BOULDERS in the low goal, and secondaily to SCALE the Tower.

Overview

  • Can cross over driving defenses in Tele-Op and autonomous (B.Moat, B.Ramparts, D.Rock Wall, D.Rough Terrain, E.Low bar)
  • Can manipulate and drive over Cheval De Frise in Tele-Op
  • Can assist in Category C defenses (Draw Bridge, Sally Port)
  • Can scale tower in ~12 seconds (time includes line up, attach, and hang)
  • Can select between multiple defenses and create new ones on the fly
  • Can score boulders in the low goal

Autonomous

Configurable autonomous modes, selected from the SmartDashboard.

  1. 1. Select Starting position (defense 1-5 or spybot)
  2. 2. Select Defense
  3. 3. Select Mode
    • Do Nothing (110% accuracy rate)
    • Cross Defense, Score Low (camera aided)
    • Cross Defense, Score Low
    • Cross Defense
    • Reach Defense
