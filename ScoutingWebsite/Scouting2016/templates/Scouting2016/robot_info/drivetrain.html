Drive Train

Mechanical System Information

  • 6 Wheel Tank Drive
    • 8 inch pneumatic wheels providing suspension
    • 4 Talon driven CIM motors (variable speed)
  • Nylon skid plates cut down on bounce and prevent getting stuck on defenses
  • Talon magnetic encoders for positioning
  • Chamfers on chassis provide clearance better exposing wheels to defenses, greatly improving rock wall efficiency
  • Plastic guards under robot to prevent dmage from defenses

Ability

  • Can go over the following defenses:
    • Category B: Moat/Ramparts
    • Category D: Rock Wall/Rough Terrain
    • Low Bar
  • Fast and Efficient (estimated 1.5 seconds to cross defenses)
  • 95% success rate on first try, 100% on second try
  • Crosses defenses front-facing only
  • Able to hold open sally port door for allliance members
